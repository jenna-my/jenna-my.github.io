Hi there! Welcome to my Github page. Below is a collection of the projects that I've worked on or am working on. Feel free to explore and visit the About Me page to get a better idea of my experiences and interests.

Projects

  • Medium.com Analytics - an NLP-focused project analyzing data from popular publishing platform Medium.com. Webscraped data from the website and investigated insights on article and user trends. Built classification models to classify text into corresponding topics. Check out our paper here. Briefly experimented with neural networks. This project was my first real exposure to deep learning!

  • Snapchat Political Ads - How much exposure can money buy you? We investigate this in our analysis of Snapchat political ad data. First part includes hypothesis/permutation testing and assessment of missingness. Second part includes model and pipeline building for prediction, parameter tuning, and fairness evaluation.
